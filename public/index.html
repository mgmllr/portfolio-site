About

Six months ago I fell in love with code. My previous career took me through many different fields and exotic landscapes: I worked as a journalist in Vietnam, interviewed dozens of the worlds most influential people at Big Think, and started a men's apparel company LIFTLUXE with two colleagues. But there was always something missing. Then last November I discovered the joys of programming and re-dedicated my life and career to learning web development. I've started with Ruby on Rails, Javascript, HTML5, and CSS3, but I am open to learning anything and everything. I am currently looking for my first job as a full-stack junior developer. I live in Brooklyn.
